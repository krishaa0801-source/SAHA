Saha's Guidelines

Policies

1 Rental Policy

At Saha's, we believe every woman deserves to look extraordinary — without buying a new outfit every time. Our rental service exists so your wardrobe has no limits.

1.1 Rental Period

  • Your rental duration is selected at the time of booking and confirmed via WhatsApp.
  • The rental period begins from the date of delivery and ends on the scheduled return date.
  • Outfits must be returned on or before the return date. Extensions are subject to availability and must be requested before confirmation of pick-up.
  • Extension charges will apply at a pro-rated daily rate based on the outfit's rental price.

1.2 Care During Rental

  • Please handle all outfits with care. Outfits are professionally cleaned and quality-checked before every delivery.
  • Only natural wear during the use of the apparel like light creasing will be acceptable. No marks, stains will be accepted.
  • Please do not alter, stitch, cut, dye, bleach, iron excessively, or modify any outfit in any way.
  • Do not attempt to wash, dry-clean, or treat the outfit yourself — return it as-is and our team will handle cleaning.

1.3 General Rules

  • Outfits are for personal use only and may not be sub-rented, lent, or transferred to another person.
  • Saha's reserves the right to refuse future rentals in cases of repeated policy violations, damage, or non-returns and also reserves the right to forfeit the deposit in such cases.
  • Every outfit is the property of Saha's or a registered Curator. Unauthorised use beyond the rental period is considered misuse.

2 Security Deposit Policy

A fully refundable pre-paid security deposit is mandatory for every rental booking.

2.1 How It Works

  • The deposit amount is the actual value of the apparel.
  • The deposit amount is specific to each outfit and will be clearly displayed before booking confirmation.
  • The deposit is collected at the time of booking, exclusive of the rental fee.
  • After the outfit is returned, it undergoes a thorough quality inspection by the Saha's team.
  • Once the outfit passes inspection with no damage beyond normal wear, your deposit is refunded in full.
  • Refunds are processed within 3–7 working days of inspection approval.

2.2 Deductions

  • In cases of damage, missing attached accessories, or embellishments, the deposit will be forfeited.
  • In case of a late return than the stipulated rental period, the additional rental charges will be deducted from the deposit amount.
  • In cases of loss or non-return, the full deposit will be forfeited.

2.3 Curator Outfits

  • For outfits listed by Curators, the deposit is still collected and managed by Saha's. The Curator does not receive the deposit directly.

3 Damage Policy

We trust our customers to enjoy outfits responsibly. This policy exists to protect both our community and our Curators.

3.1 What Counts as Normal Wear (No Charge)

  • Minor wrinkles or light creasing from wear
  • Small, washable surface marks
  • Natural fabric movement or minor pilling on older garments

3.2 What Counts as Chargeable Damage

  • Tears, rips, or cuts — accidental or otherwise
  • Burns, scorch marks, or heat damage
  • Permanent stains (food, drink, makeup, oil, pen, etc.) that cannot be professionally removed
  • Missing embellishments, sequins, buttons, belts, or accessories
  • Broken zips, hooks, or structural damage to the garment
  • Alterations, stitching, or any modification made to the outfit
  • Unauthorised washing or dry-cleaning that damages fabric or finish
  • Threads pulled out due to apparel getting stuck in sharp, pointed objects.

3.3 Assessment Process

  • All outfits are photographed before dispatch and after return.
  • The Saha's team will assess damage objectively using pre- and post-rental photographs.
  • Customers will be informed of any chargeable damage within 24 hours of return.
  • Customers may raise a dispute by contacting us within 24 hours of being notified.

4 Cancellation Policy

We understand plans change. Here's how cancellations work at Saha's.

4.1 Cancellation Windows

  • Cancellations are accepted upto only 4 hrs from the booking time.
  • Orders may be dispatched within 24 hours of booking confirmation — so please act quickly if you need to cancel.
  • Once dispatched, cancellations are not accepted and the rental fee is non-refundable.

4.2 Refund on Cancellation

  • If cancelled before dispatch: full rental fee refund + full security deposit will be refund.
  • If cancelled after dispatch: rental fee is non-refundable. Security deposit will be refunded after the outfit has reached the facility and inspected.
  • Refund timelines depend on the original payment method and are typically processed within 3–7 working days.

4.3 No-Show / Non-Returns

  • If a customer fails to return the outfit without communication, the security deposit will be forfeited and further orders will not be accepted unless communicated to the team.

5 Return Policy

Returning your outfit is simple — here's what to do.

  • Return the outfit on or before the scheduled return date confirmed at booking.
  • Repack the outfit in the original packaging provided wherever possible — this protects the garment in transit.
  • Ensure all accessories sent with the outfit (belts, dupattas, jewellery, hangers, garment bags) are returned together.
  • Missing accessories will be treated as damage and may incur replacement charges.
  • Saha's will arrange return pickup where applicable. Details will be confirmed via WhatsApp at the time of booking.
  • Do not return outfits via any other courier unless otherwise communicated/ suggested by Saha's.

6 Fit Match & AI Measurements Policy

Saha's offers a Saha Fit Match feature to help customers find outfits that align with their body measurements.

6.1 How It Works

  • Customers may optionally enter their measurements (bust, waist, hips, height) in their Fit Passport.
  • Our system compares your measurements against the outfit's listed measurements and provides a fit compatibility indication.
  • Fit Match is a guidance tool only — it does not guarantee a perfect fit.

6.2 Customer Responsibility

  • Customers are solely responsible for the accuracy of the measurements they enter.
  • Saha's is not liable for fit issues arising from incorrect, incomplete, or outdated measurements provided by the customer.
  • Since fit preferences vary (some prefer relaxed, some prefer fitted), Saha's cannot guarantee a perfect fit for every body type and preference.
  • Customers are strongly encouraged to review the outfit's detailed measurements listed on the product page before booking.
  • No refunds or cancellations will be accepted on the grounds of fit once the outfit has been dispatched.

7 Curator Policy

Saha's allows registered Curators to list their personal outfits on the platform for rental by other users.

7.1 Becoming a Curator

  • Any individual wishing to list outfits must apply through the Become a Curator section on the website.
  • Curators must submit outfit details including: name, description, category, measurements, fabric/material, condition, care instructions, and clear photographs (front, back, detail shots).
  • Outfits are reviewed and approved by the Saha's admin team before going live. Saha's reserves the right to reject any listing that does not meet quality standards.

7.2 Curator Responsibilities

  • Curators are responsible for ensuring their outfit is in the condition described at the time of listing.
  • Any misrepresentation of outfit condition or measurements may result in the Curator's account being suspended.
  • Curators must keep their listings updated and flag any changes in outfit condition immediately.

7.3 Quality Checks

  • Before a Curator's outfit is dispatched to a renter, the Saha's team will conduct a quality check. Outfits that do not meet standards will be withheld from dispatch and the Curator will be notified.
  • After return, the outfit undergoes a second quality check before being re-listed or returned to the Curator.

7.4 Earnings & Payouts

  • Curators earn a percentage of the rental fee for each successful booking, as agreed at the time of onboarding.
  • Payouts are processed after the return inspection is complete and within the agreed timeline.

8 Hygiene Promise

Every outfit you receive from Saha's — whether owned by us or listed by a Curator — goes through our standard hygiene process before delivery:

  • Professional dry-cleaning or laundering as appropriate for the fabric
  • Quality inspection for damage, condition, and completeness
  • Fresh packaging in a clean garment bag

Your hygiene and safety are not negotiable. If you ever receive an outfit that does not meet these standards, please contact us immediately via WhatsApp and we will resolve it.

9 Liability

Saha's provides outfits strictly on a rental basis.

  • Saha's is not liable for indirect losses, consequential damages, or event-related dissatisfaction arising from outfit fit, style preference, or delivery delays caused by circumstances beyond our control (weather, courier delays, etc.).
  • Saha's is not responsible for personal injury or damage to personal property caused by use of a rented outfit.
  • Liability, if any, is limited to the rental fee paid for that specific booking.

10 Privacy Policy

Saha's collects only the information necessary to process your bookings, personalise your experience, and improve our service.

10.1 What We Collect

  • Name, contact number, and delivery address
  • Body measurements (if voluntarily entered for Fit Match)
  • Booking history and outfit preferences
  • Payment transaction references (we do not store card details)

10.2 How We Use It

  • To process and confirm your bookings
  • To provide customer support
  • To improve our platform, catalogue, and recommendations
  • To send booking updates and service communications via WhatsApp

10.3 What We Don't Do

  • We do not share your personal information to third parties
  • We do not share your data with advertisers
  • We do not use your measurements for any purpose outside of Fit Match and service improvement

10.4 Your Rights

  • You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time by contacting us via WhatsApp or email.

11 Terms of Use

By placing a booking with Saha's, you agree to the following:

  • You will provide accurate personal and measurement information.
  • You will use rented outfits responsibly and for personal use only.
  • You will return outfits on time, in the condition received, with all accessories.
  • You will follow all rental, damage, and return guidelines outlined in these policies.
  • You understand that violation of these terms may result in additional charges or suspension from the platform.

Saha's reserves the right to update these policies at any time. Customers will be notified of material changes via WhatsApp or the website.
